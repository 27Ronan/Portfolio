Full Name: Mushroom Automation with Smart Hydro-environment – E-commerce Wing.
Concept: A specialized B2C (Business-to-Consumer) digital marketplace designed to empower local mushroom growers by allowing them to sell directly to customers, bypassing traditional middlemen to maximize profit margins.

### 1. Technical Stack (Your Expertise)

As the Frontend Lead for the e-commerce side, you utilized a modern, high-performance stack:

Framework: Next.js (App Router) & React.

Language: TypeScript (for type-safe, scalable code).

Styling: Tailwind CSS (for the responsive, utility-first design).

Content Management: Sanity (Headless CMS for flexible product and data management).

Authentication: Clerk (for secure user and seller onboarding).

Design Tool: Figma (where you built the UI/UX wireframes).

### 2. Key Features (The "What You Built")

You should divide this into two perspectives: the Seller and the Consumer.

The Seller Dashboard:

Inventory Management: A custom interface for growers to add, edit, and track mushroom stock levels in real-time.

Order Processing: A streamlined flow to view new orders, update shipping statuses, and manage customer fulfillment.

Sales Analytics: An interactive dashboard with visual charts and data trends to help growers track their revenue and best-selling products.

Earnings Tracker: A transparent breakdown of total earnings and pending payments.

The Consumer Experience:

Product Discovery: A clean, searchable marketplace for various mushroom types (Oyster, Ganoderma, etc.).

Secure Checkout: A smooth, multi-step checkout process integrated with Clerk for identity verification.

### 3. The "Problem & Solution" (The UI/UX Why)

The Problem: Mushroom growers often struggle with fragmented sales channels and lack the technical tools to manage inventory or see data-driven insights.

The Solution: You built a unified "Mushroom Hub" that combines a professional storefront with a backend "command center" for sellers, making the complex process of running an online farm simple and intuitive.
